
actions.repeat_type
	0 = no repeat
	1 = repeat after # units after completeted
	2 = repeat after # units after the scheduled date
	3 = repeat at next # unit after complete
	
actions.repeat_unit
	0 = days
	1 = weeks
	2 = months
	3 = years

actions.repeat_after
	If actions.repeat_type in (1,2), unit's after scheduled/completed date
	If actions.repeat_type = 3:
		if actions.repeat_unit = 0: Day of week, 
			0 = first day of week (monday or sunday, depending on locale).
			10 = first day of month
			11 = last day of month 
		if actions.repeat_unit = 1: invalid
		if actions.repeat_unit = 2: Month in year
		if actions.repeat_unit = 2: invalid
